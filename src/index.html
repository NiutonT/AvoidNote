ABOUT US

We are Avoid Note, a team formed by composers, filmmakers, designers and producers. The aim of our project is to select, compose and produce music for any type of audiovisual work.

HOW WE WORKS

1

CREATIVE VISION

We establish a close relation with the content creators who hire our services, trying to analyze and thoroughly understand both the meaning and the aim of their work. Explore your artistic vision. Our team is comfortable with any music genre. Bring your ideas & receive ready-to-use instrumentals. Our ultimate purpose is to produce a synergy between sounds and images, portraying and reflecting carefully what each creation seeks to convey.

2

MULTIPLE REVISIONS

Often it's hard to create the right track from the first attempt for artists and producers. To this end, we count on a highly specialized team, formed by professional composers and producers who are used to work with audiovisual contents. But we won't stop until you're completely satisfied with your song.

3

EXPLOTATION RIGHTS

Get explotation rights for all custom sound that you order & receive sole ownership on it. Your music cannot be used by others.

Avoid note rises as the result of the association between composers, producers and filmmakers.
A Project focused on the integral creation of music for audiovisual works.

Our process comprises of composition, recording and production offering as a result the final product, which will then be included in the visual material. Therefore, the service we provide is:

  • Unique
  • Customized
  • Made-to-measure
